BBIT (Gujarat Technological University ) Established Since 1958

Bhailalbhai & Bhikhabhai Institute of Technology (Polytechnic) is a grant-in-aid and self Financed Institute Polytechnic managed by Charutar Vidya Mandal, Vallabh Vidyanagar. The institute was established in August 1958 with the intake capacity of 60 each in conventional programmes like Civil, Mechanical & Electrical Engg.Later on institute has started new Diploma Programme in Electronics & Communication in 1985 with 60 intakes and Diploma in Computer Engg. in 1986 with 60 intake. The institute has also started two programmes under Self Finance pattern; in the year 2001, it has started Diploma in Information Technology with 60 intake and in 2003, Diploma in Electronics & Communication with 60 intake.
